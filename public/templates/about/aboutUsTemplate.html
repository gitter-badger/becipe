What is Becipe?

Becipe is an online community for people that are passionate about making their own healthy, natural skin care products.

It's a place where experts share their experience and tips, where beginners can find out how to take their first steps and where everyone can benefit a shared resource of recipes, ingredients, tips and tricks.

Why did we start Becipe?

For a while now, we've been making our own creams, soaps and shampoos. We've learned from lots of great bloggers and writers.

But we noticed something.

While there are lots of really creative and crafty people in the do-it-yourself skin care space, there wasn't a home for people could connect and learn from each other about how to make wonderful, healthy products. We started Becipe because we think the community needs such a home and we're going to build it.

Who we are

We're a collection of technologists and skin care experts that bring a unique combination of technology chops and passion for healthy living.
